Thick Grilled Cheese

Description

The Thick Grilled Cheese is a very simple dish that will leave you full and satisfied.

Simply a classic grilled cheese with a slight twist by adding some differnt cheese and loads of bacon!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Bacon (Thick-Cut)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon in a skillet on the stove
  2. When bacon is done build your sandwich alternating layers between American cheese bacon and Mozzarella
  3. Prepare the pan for the sandwich by melting some butter in it and adding a bit of the grease left over from the bacon
  4. When butter bubbles place sandwich in pan on low heat
  5. Check darkness reguarly and flip when desired darkness is reached
  6. Enjoy!
